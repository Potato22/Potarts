Hi!

   Let me tell you a bit of myself, I'm Potato22 and you can call me Pot for short. I was born on September 22nd of 2002. I began to explore the internet world since I was still 13 and there I found the My Little Pony fandom. Tons of content of fanarts and more were made from it, most of them are great and very well made pieces of art, videos, or even music.
Until one day I asked myself

"will I ever make any such content just like other artists out there?"
I sit there and wonder.

   In the year of 2018, I met a vector artist who creates unique pony characters based on an image platform so-called Derpibooru. His name is Arifproject. He's one of the first people to give me a spark of motivation and inspiration to take a new step to make new content which is vectoring, even though I gave up several times in the process I managed to do it. Now when you say? February 3rd 2018 of course. And here's where I began my vectoring career.
